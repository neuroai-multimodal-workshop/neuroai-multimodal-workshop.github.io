Neuro for AI & AI for Neuro:
Towards Multi-Modal Natural Intelligence

January 26/27, 2026
AAAI 2026
Singapore

Workshop Overview

This workshop will bring together researchers in AI, computational neuroscience, and neuromorphic engineering to advance multimodal natural intelligence. The “Neuro → AI” theme will highlight how principles from cortical circuits inspire efficient architectures like transformers and graph models. The “AI → Neuro” theme will show how machine learning drives new insights into neural dynamics from large-scale data. By uniting biological insights with scalable AI tools, the workshop aims to bridge theory, experiment, and application.
